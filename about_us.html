HISTORY

Trinity College Nabingo is a private mixed day and boarding school located in Wakiso District, Uganda.
The school was founded in 2000 by Mr. and Mrs. Kizito.
The school is a member of the Uganda Association of Independent Schools (UAIS) and the Association of Secondary School Head Teachers of Uganda (ASSHU).
The school is also a member of the Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI).

MISSION

Trinity College Nabbingo | TRICONA school mission is to produce a focused, upright, academically sound, skilled,
socially balanced, and physically capable women to serve God and their country.

VISION

To be a highly sought-after girls' secondary school for molding all-round persons for global standards.

CORE VALUES

Trinity College Nabbingo values the following:
1. Godliness
2. Integrity
3. Respect
4. Excellence
5. Discipline
6. Teamwork
