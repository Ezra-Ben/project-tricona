Annual Sports Day

Date: April 20, 2025

Join us for a fun-filled day of sports activities, friendly competition, and community spirit at the Annual Sports Day! Whether you're an athlete or a spectator, there's something for everyone. The event will feature a variety of sports competitions, including:

- Track and field events
- Basketball matches
- Soccer games
- Relay races

Come enjoy the excitement, cheer for your favorite teams, and participate in activities designed for all ages and skill levels. It's a great opportunity to meet new people, stay active, and celebrate sportsmanship.

For more information about the event, contact the Sports Committe on our website.
