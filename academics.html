Olevel Subjects

Senior one we have subjects such as Entrepreneurship,Geography,CRE,Physics,PysicalEducation, ICT,Kiswahili,Biology,Food and nutrition,English,History,Agriculture and FineArt

In senior two, in addition to these subjects we introduce more languages like French,German,Literature,Luganda and add a business subject commerce, in addition to Entreprenership

In senior three students choose two subjects in addition to the 8 compulsory subjects, they choose based on their capabilities and previous performance in those two subjects

In senior four,we believe our students are now more conversant with the subjects and now expand on their knowledge to go deeper in their subjects

Alevel Subjects

We offer both arts and science combinations.Science combinations include BCM,PCM,PEM,MEE,BCFN MEG among others. Art combinations include HEG,LEG,HEL,HDL,DEG.For science combinations they stroke it with sub ICT, Then art students who do Economics stroke their combinations with Sub mathematics, Art students who donot do Economics stroke their combinations with Sub ICT. All Alevel students have to stroke their combinations with General Paper.
